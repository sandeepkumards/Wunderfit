While there are many reasons for this, such as lack of information available and lack of options of the right bra size, wearing bras of the wrong size can lead to a series of health problems like chronic back pain, inadequate support, feeling suffocated all day and feeling uneasy while sleeping.

Bra size calculators can help you, as one of our Wunderfit Consultant, guide your clients and help them find a right sized product from our range of comfortable bras.
